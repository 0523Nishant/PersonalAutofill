About Personal Autofill

Version: 1.0.0
Last Updated: May 2023

How to Use

Personal Autofill helps you save time by automatically filling out web forms with your information. Simply enter your details once, and let the extension handle the rest.

  1. Enter your personal information in the Profile tab
  2. Customize your autofill settings as needed
  3. Click the extension icon when visiting a form
  4. Click "Fill Current Form" to autofill

Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device. Personal Autofill does not send your information to any server or third party.
